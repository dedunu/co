Dedunu Capital (Private) Limited, registered in Sri Lanka (Reg No. PV00236498), is a family-owned investment firm with expertise in equities and real estate. We strategically focus on both Sri Lankan and international markets, aiming to identify attractive opportunities across various sectors. Our mission is to invest in sustainable businesses that show potential for growth and value creation under strong management.

Please be advised that we do not provide investment products or services, accept deposits, issue loans, underwrite insurance products, or offer investment advice to the public. These activities are strictly reserved for financial institutions that are duly authorized and regulated by the Central Bank of Sri Lanka.

For a list of authorized financial institutions, please refer to the official website of the Central Bank of Sri Lanka.
